Types Of Coffee

     
Arabica

Arabica is the most popular type of bean used for coffee. They are higher quality than Robusta, and more expensive. They result in coffee with a sweeter, less harsh taste.

Robusta

They have a higher caffeine content and taste more bitter than Arabica beans. These beans are often used to make instant coffee and espresso blends.
